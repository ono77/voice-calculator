2. Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles information when you use it. The Extension is designed to be privacy-friendly and to collect as little data as possible.

2.1. Data processed by the Extension

When you click the microphone icon, the Extension uses the browser’s built-in Web Speech API (or equivalent speech recognition engine) to convert your voice into text. This process is handled by your browser and/or the underlying platform, and may rely on external services provided by the browser vendor (for example, Google services in Chrome).

The Extension itself uses the recognized text only to:

  • Try to interpret the spoken expression as a mathematical operation (e.g. “27.4 divided by 4.3”).
  • Display the recognized text in the popup UI.
  • Calculate and display the result locally in the popup.

2.2. No collection of personal data by the Extension

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data such as your name, email address, account details, or browsing history.

In particular:

  • The recognized speech text is used only in memory, for the duration of the current calculation.
  • No transcript is sent to the developer’s servers.
  • No analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising networks are integrated in the Extension.

2.3. Microphone access

The Extension only requests access to the microphone when you explicitly click the microphone icon in the popup. Permission to use the microphone is managed by your browser. You can allow or deny this permission, and you can revoke it at any time via your browser’s site/extension settings.

Audio input is processed by the browser’s speech recognition engine. The Extension does not record or store audio itself.

2.4. Permissions used by the Extension

The Extension may request certain Chrome permissions such as:

  • activeTab – to interact with the current tab if necessary for voice activation.
  • microphone (via browser prompt) – to allow speech recognition when you choose to use voice input.
  • storage – possibly used to store simple settings or preferences locally in your browser.

These permissions are used solely to provide the voice calculator functionality and related options. The Extension does not use them to track you or profile your activity.

2.5. Third-party services

Speech recognition is performed using the browser’s own implementation of the Web Speech API or an equivalent platform feature. This may involve third-party services (for example, Google’s speech recognition systems in Chrome). Data sent to such third parties is handled according to their own privacy policies.

You should consult your browser vendor’s privacy policy for more information on how speech data may be processed by the underlying platform.

2.6. Children’s privacy

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child is using the Extension in a way that exposes personal data, you can remove the Extension from the browser at any time.
